Background

The background of the game is that two wizards come to another world through the portal, but as time goes by, the energy of the portal can only send one wizard back. For this reason, the two wizards confront each other through magic cards. In the game, the player plays the role of one wizard and defeats the opponent, thus gaining the opportunity to return to the original world through the portal.The background of the game is that two wizards come to another world through the portal, but as time goes by, the energy of the portal can only send one wizard back. Introduction

Goal

  • Control the game character to get cards (randomly generated on the ground);
  • Draw shapes on the card in sequence to active the card;
  • Get enough game points or let the opponent player's health point to zero.

How to play

Keyboard

Player1

  • A/D/W/S control game character to move toward four directions (left/right/up/down)
  • E draw square
  • R draw upper triangle
  • T draw lower triangle
  • Y clear the shapes

Player2

  • J/L/I/K control game character to move toward four directions (left/right/up/down)
  • O draw square
  • P draw upper triangle
  • [ draw lower triangle
  • ] clear the shapes


Arduino + Accelerometer

  • Move the accelerometer to control the game character;
  • Press the button to ready to draw the shapes;
  • Move the accelerometer to draw the shapes to active the card.

Single Player Mode

Medusa

Description

Medusa changes her position randomly and then shots arrows or gazes the enemy. Player will be damaged when being shot and will be damaged and frozen for several seconds when facing toward Medusa.

Features

For Medusa

  • Max HP: 50
  • Max GP: 50
  • Rage: Deal double damage when HP under 20
  • Hunter: Double arrow speed when GP over 25

For player

  • Max HP: 20
  • Max GP: 50
  • Accelerate: 125% moving speed when GP over 15
  • Bless: Restore 1 HP while using a card when GP over 30
